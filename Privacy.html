Siyaasadda Qarsoodiga (Privacy Policy)

La cusboonaysiiyay / Last updated: Abriil 15, 2025

SAHAN DATA waxay ka go'an tahay ilaalinta xogta gaarka ah ee isticmaalayaasheeda. Siyaasaddani waxay sharraxaysaa sida aan u ururino, u isticmaalno, una ilaalino macluumaadkaaga.

📋 1. Xogta Aan Uruurino

  • Macluumaadka Diiwaangelinta: Magaca, lambarka taleefanka, email-ka, degmada, iskuulka, iyo koodhka kooxda.
  • Xogta Dalabka: Alaabta aad iibsato, habka bixinta, iyo taariikhda iibka.
  • Xogta Tixraacyada: Koodhka tixraacaaga iyo dadka aad soo martiqaaday.

🔒 2. Sida Aan U Isticmaalno Xogta

  • In aan kuu sahlo diiwaangelinta, galitaanka, iyo iibka.
  • In aan kuugu xidhno abaalmarinnada iyo tartamada.
  • In aan kuugu soo dirno ogeysiisyada muhiimka ah (sida xaqiijinta dalabka).
  • In aan horumarino adeegyadeena oo aan falanqayno isticmaalka.

🤝 3. Xogta Aan La Wadaagno Dadka Saddexaad

Ma iibinno, ma awoodinno, mana wadaaginno xogtaada shakhsiyeed qof saddexaad, marka laga reebo:

  • Haddii ay sharci ku qasabayso (maxkamad ama hay'ad dowladeed).
  • Si loo fuliyo dalabkaaga (tusaale: la xiriirista shirkadda isgaadhsiinta si loo bixiyo data).

🍪 4. Kukiyada (Cookies)

Waxaan isticmaalnaa kukiyada si aan u xasuusanno doorashooyinkaaga iyo in aan falanqayno sida website-ka loo isticmaalo. Waad ka hadli kartaa goobaha browser-ka.

🛡️ 5. Amniga Xogta

Waxaan adeegsanaa Supabase oo leh habab ammaan sida RLS iyo SSL si looga hortago gelitaanka aan la ogolayn. Si kastaba ha ahaatee, lama xaqiijin karo gabaabso ammaan.

👶 6. Xogta Carruurta

Website-keena loogama talagal carruurta da'doodu ka yar tahay 13. Kama ururin si ula kac ah xogta qofka da'daas.

📞 7. Xiriir

Haddii aad qabto su'aalo ku saabsan siyaasaddan, fadlan nala soo xiriir:

  • WhatsApp: +252 61 253 8844
  • Email: support@sahandata.com

Waxaan kuu soo jeedinnaa inaad si joogto ah ula socoto siyaasaddan, sababtoo ah waxaa laga yaabaa inay isbeddesho.

SAHAN DATA is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

📋 1. Information We Collect

  • Registration data: Name, phone number, email, district, school, group code.
  • Order data: Products purchased, payment method, purchase history.
  • Referral data: Your referral code and people you referred.

🔒 2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enable registration, login, and purchases.
  • To manage rewards and competitions.
  • To send important notifications (order confirmations).
  • To improve our services and analyse usage.

🤝 3. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information except:

  • When required by law (court order or government agency).
  • To fulfill your order (e.g., contacting the mobile operator to deliver data).

🍪 4. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and analyse usage. You can disable them in your browser.

🛡️ 5. Data Security

We use Supabase with RLS and SSL to prevent unauthorised access. However, no system is 100% secure.

👶 6. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from them.
