Platform Policy

1. Data Protection Policy

SAHAN DATA is committed to protecting your personal information. We collect only necessary data (name, phone, email, district, school) to provide our services. Your data is never sold to third parties. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

2. Fair Usage Policy

To ensure fair competition, we prohibit any form of cheating, including but not limited to: creating multiple accounts, using bots to generate referrals, or artificially inflating order counts. Violations will result in account suspension and forfeiture of all rewards.

3. Refund & Cancellation Policy

All purchases of data packages are final. Once the USSD code is dialed and the data is activated, no refunds will be issued. If you experience technical issues, contact support within 24 hours for assistance.

4. Reward Redemption Policy

Bonus points can be redeemed for future purchases or converted to data packages (subject to terms). Free data packages awarded as prizes must be claimed within 30 days; otherwise, they expire. Certificates are issued digitally and can be downloaded from your profile.

5. Account Deletion Policy

You may request account deletion by emailing support@sahandata.com. Upon deletion, your personal data will be removed, but transactional records may be retained for legal purposes. Any unused bonus points or pending rewards will be forfeited.

6. Communication Policy

By registering, you consent to receive transactional emails and SMS regarding orders, rewards, and important updates. You may opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Age Restriction Policy

Users under 18 years of age must have parental or guardian consent to use the platform. SAHAN DATA reserves the right to verify age and suspend accounts that violate this policy.

8. Modification of Policies

We may update these policies from time to time. Continued use of the platform after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policies.
